About

I serve as an Assistant Professor at Istanbul University Faculty of Aquatic Sciences. My research focuses on aquaculture, aquaponics, integrated multi-trophic aquaculture (IMTA), microplastic pollution, fish reproductive physiology and swimming physiology. I have taken part in TÜBİTAK-, COST- and BAP-supported projects as principal investigator and researcher. I have published numerous scientific articles, book chapters and national/international conference papers in the field of aquatic sciences.
